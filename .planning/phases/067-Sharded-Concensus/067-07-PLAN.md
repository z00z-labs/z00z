# 067-07 Validator And Theorem Binding

<objective>
Make downstream acceptance require the quorum-certificate artifact by binding
its digest or reference into local DA publication, carrying that binding through
resolve, and rejecting validator paths where certificate, theorem, publication,
and ordered batch do not agree on one subject.
</objective>
