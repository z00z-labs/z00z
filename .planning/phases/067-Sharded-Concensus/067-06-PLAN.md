# 067-06 Join Removal And Rotation Simulation

<objective>
Make membership transitions safe by proving observer admission, readiness,
planned primary rotation, emergency takeover, and member removal under the same
subject-digest, lineage, term, and generation rules as the live shard-local
quorum path.
</objective>
