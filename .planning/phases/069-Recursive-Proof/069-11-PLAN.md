  parameter metadata, and `069-BENCHMARKS.md` local evidence/stop decision that

## acceptance_tests

- Serialized byte counts recompute from persisted artifacts and separately report
  PP/PK, VK/verifier bundle, and proof-envelope/network payload.
- Timing units, sample counts, build profile, hardware/toolchain, fixture identity, backend/parameter/context/predicate digests, and peak memory are present.
- Imported numbers are labeled targets only; measured caps/latency determine pass or stop/split.

## simulation_gate

- No imported or synthetic performance closes. Benchmarks execute real local provers/verifiers; only fixture inputs are deterministic.

## negative_tests

- Missing units/field/digest/profile/fixture, mismatched chain/epoch/backend, recomputation mismatch, zero samples, imported number presented as result, cap/latency/resource breach hidden.
