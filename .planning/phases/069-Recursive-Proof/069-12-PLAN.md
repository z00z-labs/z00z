## acceptance_tests

- Real deterministic-input 3/5 Nova chains and bounded real Plonky3 epoch verify.
- Height 999 reports open epoch/no anchor; height 1000 requires complete proof/manifest/PQ anchor only at live enforcement.
- Tamper/reorg/restart/resource/archive/audit/snapshot/pruning/non-authority
  scenarios pass, including actual Nova and Plonky3 verifier rejection under
  changed network, genesis, config, and predicate context.
- The shared context replay matrix contains exactly eight mandatory rows:
  `CTX-NETWORK-{NOVA,PLONKY3}`, `CTX-GENESIS-{NOVA,PLONKY3}`,
  `CTX-CONFIG-{NOVA,PLONKY3}`, and
  `CTX-PREDICATE-{NOVA,PLONKY3}`. Each row must reach the corresponding actual
  pinned verifier and record typed rejection independently of envelope checks.
- Every additional shared predicate/witness mutation declared by
  `.planning/phases/069-Recursive-Proof/069-PREDICATE.md` has one Nova and one Plonky3 execution row; missing or
  duplicate backend rows fail the scenario audit.
- The inherited-live distributed HJMT matrix is mandatory: replication, quorum,
  conflict resolution, standby catch-up, route rollout, dispatch, membership,
  restart, partition/heal, stale lineage, divergent roots, and failure telemetry
  each execute through existing components and persist one typed evidence pointer.
- Cadence failures translate exactly to `HybridCadenceMismatch` and
  `PqAnchorMissing`, including the 999 negative/1000 positive boundary.
- Wallet-boundary scenario gives a wallet only finality certificate, committed
  root, and HJMT membership proof; it proves inclusion without PP, PK, VK, or a
  recursive sidecar. Validator/watcher verifier scenarios cache the generation-
  pinned VK once and never receive prover material.
- The simulator suite emits and reloads the required 3-step/5-step sidecars,
  real Nova and Plonky3 proof evidence, tampered-middle and tampered-witness
  rejects, canonical-admission invariance, local-spike measurements, cadence
  and PQ-anchor evidence, three independent archive receipts, unpinned-IPFS
  rejection, snapshot evidence, and before/after pruning decisions.

## simulation_gate

- Fake only external transport, remote process boundary, external DA transport,
  unavailable third-party network, wall clock, or fault scheduler. Always use
  real cryptography, package verification, planner/route output, HJMT journal/state,
  storage commit/recovery, publication bindings, validator/watcher checks, and
  per-component state. The distributed HJMT matrix is unconditional and uses no
  simulator-owned parallel state model.
- Required live scenario IDs are exactly `HJMT-REPLICATION`, `HJMT-QUORUM`,
  `HJMT-CONFLICT-RESOLUTION`, `HJMT-STANDBY-CATCHUP`, `HJMT-ROUTE-ROLLOUT`,

  `HJMT-PARTITION-HEAL`, `HJMT-STALE-LINEAGE`, `HJMT-DIVERGENT-ROOTS`, and
  `HJMT-FAILURE-TELEMETRY`.
