## Transcript and entropy reachability

Ordinary NIFS absorbs the public-parameter digest, relies on the augmented
`U2.X[0]` binding of `U1`, absorbs `U2` and `comm_T`, then squeezes the fixed
challenge. Relaxed NIFS separately absorbs the verifier/public-parameter
binding, both `U1` and `U2`, and `comm_T`. The shortened ordinary transcript is
therefore confined to the upstream augmented-circuit path and is not exposed
as a reusable project folding helper.

Production entropy is selected inside the pinned dependency. The reached
`RecursiveSNARK` initialization/step, ordinary and relaxed NIFS blinding, and
random relaxed-instance witnesses call `rand_core::OsRng`. The project owner
accepts no RNG, seed, transcript, Poseidon mode, curve, commitment, or Spartan
backend argument. Test fixtures may be deterministic only before entering the
dependency-owned proof sampler.

## A-17 disposition

Audit 2 Sections 21, 23, and 24 identify the polynomial-depth Nova analysis
cited by this phase and the additional algebraic-group, Fiat-Shamir/hash,
adaptive-query, discrete-log, and polynomial-depth premises that must match the
pinned construction. Repository tests can pin source, reachability, transcript
shape, parameters, entropy sites, step counts, and unchanged-verifier behavior;
they cannot prove those external reduction premises.

The repository authority accepted this exact residual conditionally on
2026-07-20 under decision reference
`phase-069-t2-interactive-authority-2026-07-20`. Therefore A-17 remains
explicit and conditional. The project may report the
local root-counting term `Q_fold / 2^127` only as one term in the conditional
responsibility bound. It must not report an unconditional “128-bit cumulative
IVC security” claim. Changing the dependency source, features, backend,
Poseidon modes, transcript, sampling sites, or proof-depth policy reopens this
ledger and blocks T2 acceptance.
