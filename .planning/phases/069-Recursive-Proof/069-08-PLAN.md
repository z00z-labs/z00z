# 069-08 Recursive Plonky3 Epoch Aggregation

<objective>
Implement strict storage-owned epoch statement/proof/manifest schemas and a real
recursive Plonky3 aggregation proof over exactly the configured ordered canonical
range, with real leaf count/tree shape and independent canonical predicate proof
rather than Nova-only wrapping. Add a separate cross-epoch rolling history proof
so bounded proof retention is a proven theorem rather than a storage assumption.

## acceptance_tests

- Exact consecutive range, height math, real leaf count, tree shape, context/predicate/parameter digests, and ordered roots verify.
- Missing/reordered/duplicate/padded/mixed-parameter leaf, changed tree/height/count/proof, Nova-only wrapper, cross-context replay, and cap overflow reject.
- A history successor verifies the previous history proof and the exact next
  epoch proof, enforces contiguous roots/ranges and monotonic history length,
  and binds the updated epoch-anchor/MMR root. Digest-only predecessor links,
  skipped/repeated epochs, reset-to-genesis, mixed generation, and unproven
  parameter rotation reject.
- Canonical epoch close/finality does not wait for either recursive artifact;
  missing evidence creates a bounded shadow backlog and pruning hold, never a
  canonical rejection or rollback.
- Every epoch/history/rotation object dispatches through its exact registry row;
  a rotation changes authority/parameter generation without silently changing
  codec meaning or resetting cumulative soundness history.

## rolling_history_contract

`Plonky3EpochProofV2` proves exactly one configured epoch. It MUST NOT be
described as a proof of all prior epochs. Bounded cryptographic history requires
the following separate construction:

- `HistoryAccumulatorStatementV2` public inputs MUST bind chain context,
  history version, first/last epoch and height, history length, genesis/base
  state root, previous/current terminal state roots, previous/current compact
  epoch-anchor/MMR roots, exact new epoch statement/proof digests, predicate/AIR
  digest, and verifier-parameter generation.
- The base case MUST bind the configured genesis/checkpoint trust anchor and the
  first verified epoch. It MUST be a distinct tagged branch; an ordinary
  successor cannot set history length to zero or recreate the base case.
- The successor circuit MUST cryptographically verify the previous
  `Plonky3HistoryProofV2` and the exact next `Plonky3EpochProofV2`; enforce
  `previous.end_root == epoch.start_root`, consecutive epoch/height math,
  strictly incremented history length, and the exact new anchor/MMR update; and
  expose only the new terminal public inputs. Checking predecessor digests or
  native verifier booleans is forbidden.
- Same-generation recursion MUST use one authority-pinned recursion manifest.
  A parameter/AIR/transcript rotation requires `HistoryRotationBridgeV2`, which
  verifies the old terminal history proof and the first new-generation epoch,
  binds old/new VK/manifest digests, activation height, context, predicate,
  terminal/start roots, and the authority-approved rotation commitment. Until
  the bridge verifies, both generations and all affected proof bodies remain
  retained and pruning is blocked.
- The pinned recursion/security manifest MUST define the concrete per-proof
  soundness-error bound, conservative composition rule, finite accepted-epoch
  budget for the generation, inherited cumulative error bound, and minimum
  residual security target. The history statement MUST bind a monotonic,
  overflow-checked accepted-epoch count. A literal `security_bits: 124` is only
  a per-proof target and MUST NOT be described as eternal history security.
  Rotation through `HistoryRotationBridgeV2` carries the inherited cumulative
  error forward; merely wrapping an old proof MUST NOT reset it. Before the
  configured budget is exhausted, governance MUST activate parameters that
  preserve the total residual target, perform a full sound re-proof from an
  independently retained basis, or stop adding recursive evidence. Canonical
  checkpoint admission remains available in the last case.
- The proof remains non-authoritative shadow evidence. Its absence/failure MUST
  hold evidence promotion and deletion only; it MUST NOT invalidate an already
  certified checkpoint.
- Storage SHOULD keep the current and two preceding verified history-proof
  bodies plus every proof required by an unresolved challenge or rotation.
  Exact epoch/base proof bodies become prune-eligible only after a verified
  history successor/bridge, the Plan 09 height window, and a complete pruning
  ticket. Compact epoch anchors and proof/manifest digests remain permanent.
- Plan 11 MUST measure proof/prover/verifier/RSS growth across at least 1, 2, 3,
  10, and 100 successor folds and recompute cumulative soundness. Any
  unbounded proof, verifier, recursion-depth, or security-loss behavior triggers
  stop/split and forbids the bounded-retention claim.
