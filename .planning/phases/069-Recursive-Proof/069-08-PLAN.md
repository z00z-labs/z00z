<objective>
Implement strict storage-owned epoch statement/proof/manifest schemas and a real
recursive Plonky3 aggregation proof over exactly the configured ordered canonical
range, with real leaf count/tree shape and independent canonical predicate proof
rather than Nova-only wrapping.
